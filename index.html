Kitty Microbiome Project

Explore your cat!

Every animal has its own unique diversity of bacteria in its digestive tract. Cats are no exception. How does this composition influence your feline companion?s health? That is the question that the Kitty Microbiome Project is trying to address. This web app allows you to explore the four most common bacterial phyla found in your cat and compare them with other cats.
